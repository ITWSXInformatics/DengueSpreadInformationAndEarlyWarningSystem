About

This project was initiated as part of the X-Informatics course at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute during Spring 2020. The project, under the guidance of Prof. Thilanka Munasinghe, was developed as a Python based (Flask) application and later compiled into a published research. The paper was submitted to ICMHI 2020 and is available via ACM.
